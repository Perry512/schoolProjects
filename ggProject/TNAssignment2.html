Guilty Gear -Strive-

Guilty Gear -Strive- is the 5th mainline addition to the popular fighting game series, "Guilty Gear"

You can play as many classic charcters such as;
  • Sol Badguy - The Relentless Striker
  • Ky Kiske - The Almighty All-Rounder
  • May - The Relentless Girl
  • Zato-1 - The Techincal Shadow Warrior
  • Milia Rage - The Forbidden Hair Assassin
  • Jack-O - The Hyper Energetic Mashal Fighter
  • Leo Whitefang - The Twinblade All-Rounder
  • Chipp Zanuff - The Godspeed Ninja

The game also includes many newcoming fighters, my favorites being;
  1. Nagoriyuki - The Noble Vampire Samurai
  2. Goldlewis Dickinson - The Secretary of Absolute Defense
  3. Giovanna - The Superpowered Sercret Service Agent
  4. Anji Mito - The Elegant Martial Dancer
  5. Bridget - The Free-form Tricky Spinner
  6. Happy Chaos - The Gunslinging Broken Messiah
  7. Testament - The Elegant Grim Reaper
  8. Delilah - The Unknown Girl
